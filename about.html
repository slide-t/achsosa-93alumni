About Army Children High School, Epe, Lagos, Nigeria.

Our Story

Army Children High School, Epe, Lagos, Nigeria has stood as a beacon of knowledge, discipline, and excellence since its founding. It was established with a mission to nurture both academic brilliance and moral integrity in young minds, providing them with the foundation to excel in life.

Over the decades, the school has produced outstanding alumni who have made significant contributions across various fields—education, business, technology, military service, and beyond. The values instilled in the classrooms continue to guide and inspire each generation.

Our alumni association, ACHSOSA ’93 Set, carries forward this legacy by uniting former students and supporting the growth and development of the school community. Through projects, events, and charitable initiatives, we remain committed to giving back and keeping the bond strong.

Today, Army Children High School, Epe, Lagos (situtated within the hills), now, relocated uphills, is not just a school—it is a family, a tradition, and a legacy of excellence that lives on in every student who has walked its halls.
