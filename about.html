About ACHSOSA '93 Set

The Army Children High School Old Students Association (ACHSOSA) ’93 Set is a vibrant community of proud alumni connected by cherished memories, lifelong friendships, and the values instilled in us by our alma mater.

We are committed to supporting one another, giving back to our school, and uplifting our community. Our collective efforts extend beyond nostalgia—they focus on building opportunities, mentoring the younger generation, and contributing meaningfully to society.

Our guiding principle is simple: "Once a student, forever a family." As the ’93 Set, we honor the past, celebrate our present, and lay the foundation for a brighter future.

With shared passion and unity, we continue to uphold the legacy of ACHSOSA— one of excellence, resilience, and service.
