About ACHSOSA '93 Set

Together, we honor our past, celebrate our present, and shape a brighter future.

Who We Are

The Anglican Comprehensive High School Old Students Association (ACHSOSA) ’93 Set is a community of proud alumni bound by shared memories, values, and a commitment to excellence. We are united not just by the year we graduated, but by the lifelong bonds of friendship and the common goal of giving back to the institution that shaped us.

Our mission is to foster unity among members, support one another in professional and personal growth, and contribute to the development of our alma mater and community.

With a foundation built on discipline, resilience, and service, the ’93 Set continues to stand as a beacon of inspiration. We believe that together, we can create opportunities, uplift future generations, and ensure that the legacy of ACHSOSA endures.

Once a student, forever a family. Together, we are stronger.
