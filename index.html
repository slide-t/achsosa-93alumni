ACHSOSA '93 SET Alumni Association is a vibrant and dedicated community of graduates from Army Children High School Epe. Our members are committed to fostering lifelong connections, supporting the growth of our alma mater, and contributing positively to society through service, leadership, and collaborative initiatives. We celebrate our shared heritage while inspiring current and future generations to excel.

School Anthem (Sample)

Vision: "To cultivate a thriving and united alumni community that upholds the legacy of Army Children High School Epe, inspires lifelong learning, and positively impacts society through service, leadership, and innovation."

Mission: "ACHSOSA '93 SET Alumni exists to connect and empower graduates, support the development of our alma mater, foster professional and personal growth among members, promote community engagement, and preserve the traditions and values of Army Children High School Epe."
